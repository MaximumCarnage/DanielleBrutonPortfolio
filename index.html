Games! Glorious Games!

Here are a few of my most polished titles for your perusual, Click Here To see my full library of Games and other media i have created

Unity:A Pirate's Duel

A Pirates Duel was a group project i undertook with several friends during a Peterborough Game Jam, and is a multiplayer networked 1v1 pirate battle.The game takes place atop a ship in a pirate fleet, and requires the player to kill thier opponent using slashes and blocks.

Android:Get Toasted

Get Toasted is my first large Android game, starring a very enthusiastic piece of toast on his journey across the kitchen table. The game functions as a infinite runner, with enemies increasing in speed, with a variety of different enemies

Unity:Joust Recreated

Very early in my Game Development Education, i attended the SemiAnnual Peterborough Game Jam. I was still unfamiliar with unity, yet was able to create a very solid recreation of the arcade classic Joust. At the time of first making it the main jousting mechanic was unfinished,yet enemies would be killed and new wave would spawn after they all died, incrementing score as they die.
